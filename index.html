What I Do

Community and Education

I lead fruit-focused community programs and teach hands-on grafting workshops that build local food resilience and practical growing skills.

Orchard Design and Stewardship

I design orchard systems and manage planting, maintenance, and seasonal planning for productive, well-kept landscapes.

Tree Health and Arboriculture

I support tree health, structure, and safety through informed pruning, diagnostics, and long-term care strategies.

Plant Science, Breeding, and Rare Fruit Research

I apply plant science, breeding, and field observation methods to select resilient, high-quality fruit varieties, and I trial rare cultivars for real-world performance and flavor in coastal California and beyond.
